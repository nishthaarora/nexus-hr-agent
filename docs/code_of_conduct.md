# Code of Conduct at Nexus HR

## Our Values
Nexus HR is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace. All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity regardless of role, background, or seniority.

## Respectful Workplace
Harassment, discrimination, and bullying of any kind are not tolerated. This includes verbal, written, and digital communication. Offensive language, exclusionary jokes, and unwanted physical contact are all violations of this policy. Violations may result in immediate termination.

## Reporting Misconduct
Employees who experience or witness misconduct should report it through the Nexus HR portal under HR > Report Misconduct. Reports can be made anonymously. All reports are investigated within 10 business days. Retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report is a serious violation and grounds for termination.

## Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any personal relationships or financial interests that could conflict with their responsibilities at Nexus HR. Disclosures are made through HR > Conflict of Interest in the portal. Examples include hiring a family member, investing in a client company, or working for a competitor.

## Confidentiality
Employees must not share confidential company information, client data, or colleague personal information outside the organisation. This obligation continues after employment ends. Breaches of confidentiality may result in legal action.

## Use of Company Resources
Company resources — including devices, software, and time — must be used for business purposes. Limited personal use is acceptable provided it does not interfere with work. Accessing inappropriate content on company devices is a violation of this policy.

## Social Media
Employees must not post confidential information or make statements that could damage Nexus HR's reputation on social media. Personal opinions expressed online must be clearly identified as personal and not representative of the company.
