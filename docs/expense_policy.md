# Expense Policy at Nexus HR

## Overview
Nexus HR reimburses employees for reasonable business expenses incurred in the course of their work. All expenses must be pre-approved where required and submitted with valid receipts.

## Expense Categories and Limits
- **Travel (flights):** Requires manager approval before booking. Economy class only for flights under 6 hours.
- **Accommodation:** Up to $200 per night. Any hotel above this limit requires director approval.
- **Meals:** Up to $50 per person per day when traveling. Client entertainment meals up to $100 per person require manager approval.
- **Ground transport:** Taxis and rideshares are reimbursable. Personal vehicle use is reimbursed at $0.65 per mile.
- **Home office:** Up to $500 per year for equipment. Requires receipts and manager approval.
- **Training and conferences:** Requires pre-approval from manager and HR. Covered in full if pre-approved.

## Submitting Expenses
Submit all expense claims through the Nexus HR portal under Finance > Expenses within 30 days of incurring the expense. Claims submitted after 30 days may not be reimbursed. Attach original receipts for all items over $25. Credit card statements are not accepted as receipts.

## Approval Process
Expenses under $500 are approved by your direct manager. Expenses between $500 and $2000 require director approval. Expenses over $2000 require VP approval. Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following are not reimbursable: alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment meal), personal items, fines and penalties, first-class travel, and expenses without receipts.
