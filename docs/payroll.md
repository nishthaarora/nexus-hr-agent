# Payroll at Nexus HR

## Pay Schedule
Nexus HR pays employees on a bi-weekly schedule. Pay periods run Monday to Sunday and salaries are deposited every second Friday. If a pay date falls on a public holiday, payment is made on the preceding Thursday.

## Payslips
Digital payslips are available in the Nexus HR portal under the Payroll section on every pay date. Payslips include a breakdown of gross salary, tax deductions, benefits deductions, and net pay. Employees can download payslips as PDFs for their records.

## Tax Information
Nexus HR automatically withholds federal and state income tax based on the information provided in your employee profile. To update your tax withholding, navigate to Payroll > Tax Settings in the portal and submit a new W-4 form. Changes take effect in the next pay cycle.

## Bank Details
Bank account details for direct deposit are managed under Payroll > Bank Details in the portal. Changes to bank details must be submitted at least 5 business days before the next pay date to take effect in that cycle. Otherwise changes apply to the following cycle.

## Salary Advances
Employees may request a salary advance of up to 50% of their monthly salary once per calendar year. Requests must be submitted through the portal and approved by HR. The advance is deducted from the following month's paycheck.

## Payroll Errors
If you believe there is an error in your paycheck, contact HR through the portal within 5 business days of the pay date. Verified errors are corrected in the next pay cycle. For urgent corrections, contact payroll@nexushr.com directly.
