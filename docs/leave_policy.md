# Leave Policy at Nexus HR

## Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of annual leave per year, earned at 1.67 days per month. Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata basis. Leave balances reset on January 1st and unused leave above 5 days cannot be carried over to the following year.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not carry over. For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate is required. Submit the certificate to HR through the portal within 5 business days of returning to work.

## Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of fully paid parental leave. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption date. Notify HR at least 8 weeks in advance where possible.

## Requesting Leave
All leave requests must be submitted through the Nexus HR portal under the Leave section. Requests require manager approval. Annual leave requests should be submitted at least 5 business days in advance. Emergency leave can be approved retroactively within 2 business days.

## Public Holidays
Nexus HR observes all federal public holidays. Employees required to work on a public holiday will receive a substitute day off to be taken within 30 days.

## Unpaid Leave
Employees may request unpaid leave for personal reasons after exhausting their annual leave balance. Unpaid leave requires approval from both the direct manager and HR. Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance.
