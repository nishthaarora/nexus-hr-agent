# Employee Offboarding at Nexus HR

## Resignation Process
Employees wishing to resign must submit written notice through the Nexus HR portal under HR > Resignation. The standard notice period is 4 weeks for individual contributors and 8 weeks for managers and above. Notice periods may be negotiated with HR and the direct manager.

## Final Paycheck
The final paycheck is issued on the next regular pay date following the last day of employment. It includes all outstanding salary, accrued but unused annual leave (up to the carry-over cap), and any approved outstanding expense claims.

## Equipment Return
All company equipment must be returned on or before the last day of employment. IT will provide a return shipping label for remote employees. Unreturned equipment will be deducted from the final paycheck at replacement cost.

## System Access
All system access is revoked at 5pm on the employee's last day. Employees should ensure personal files and contacts are saved before this time. Company email is deactivated 24 hours after the last day. A forwarding message is set up for 30 days.

## Exit Interview
HR will schedule an exit interview during the final week of employment. The interview is voluntary but encouraged. Feedback is used to improve the employee experience and is kept confidential from the direct manager.

## References
Nexus HR provides employment verification letters confirming dates of employment and role title. Reference letters commenting on performance are provided at the manager's discretion. Reference requests should be directed to hr@nexushr.com.

## Non-Disclosure
Employees are reminded that non-disclosure agreements signed at onboarding remain in effect after employment ends. Questions about NDA obligations should be directed to legal@nexushr.com.
