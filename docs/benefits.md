# Employee Benefits at Nexus HR

## Health Insurance
Nexus HR covers 80% of the premium for employee health insurance and 60% for dependents. Three plan tiers are available: Basic, Standard, and Premium. Plan selection is made during onboarding and can be changed during the annual open enrollment period in November or within 30 days of a qualifying life event such as marriage or birth of a child.

## Dental and Vision
Dental and vision coverage are available as optional add-ons. Nexus HR covers 50% of dental premiums and 50% of vision premiums. Dental covers two cleanings per year and up to $1500 in annual restorative care. Vision covers one eye exam per year and up to $200 toward glasses or contact lenses.

## Retirement Plan
Nexus HR offers a 401(k) plan through Fidelity. The company matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary. Employees are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment. Vesting is immediate for employee contributions and on a 3-year graded schedule for employer match contributions.

## Mental Health Support
All employees have access to 12 free counseling sessions per year through the Employee Assistance Program (EAP). Sessions are confidential and can be accessed through the EAP portal or by calling 1-800-NEXUS-EAP. The EAP also provides legal and financial advisory services.

## Learning and Development
Nexus HR provides $2000 per year per employee for learning and development. This covers online courses, certifications, books, and conference attendance. Requests are submitted through the portal under HR > Learning Budget and require manager approval. Unused budget does not carry over.

## Wellness Benefit
Employees receive a $500 annual wellness stipend that can be used for gym memberships, fitness equipment, meditation apps, or other wellness-related expenses. Claims are submitted through Finance > Wellness Expenses with receipts.
