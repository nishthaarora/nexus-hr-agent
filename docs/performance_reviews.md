# Performance Reviews at Nexus HR

## Review Cycle
Nexus HR conducts performance reviews twice a year — mid-year in July and annual in December. New employees receive their first review at the 6-month mark regardless of the review cycle timing.

## Self Assessment
Two weeks before each review, employees receive a self-assessment form in the portal under Performance > My Reviews. The self-assessment covers goal progress, key accomplishments, challenges faced, and development areas. Self-assessments must be submitted at least 5 business days before the scheduled review meeting.

## Manager Review
Managers complete a performance rating in the portal after reviewing the employee's self-assessment. Ratings are on a 5-point scale: Unsatisfactory, Needs Improvement, Meets Expectations, Exceeds Expectations, and Outstanding. Managers must provide written comments justifying the rating.

## Goals Setting
During the review meeting, managers and employees agree on goals for the next review period. Goals are entered into the portal under Performance > Goals and must follow the SMART framework — Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. A minimum of 3 goals must be set per cycle.

## Performance Improvement Plans
Employees rated Unsatisfactory or Needs Improvement for two consecutive review cycles are placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP). The PIP is a 90-day structured plan with specific milestones. HR and the direct manager co-own the PIP process.

## Salary Reviews
Annual salary adjustments are tied to the December performance review. Employees rated Meets Expectations receive a cost-of-living adjustment. Exceeds Expectations and Outstanding ratings are eligible for merit increases of up to 10% subject to budget approval.
