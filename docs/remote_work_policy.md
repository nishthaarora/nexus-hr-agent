# Remote Work Policy at Nexus HR

## Eligibility
Remote work is available to all full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period and whose role does not require physical presence. Part-time and contract employees may work remotely subject to manager approval.

## Hybrid Work Model
Nexus HR operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in the office a minimum of 2 days per week. Specific in-office days are agreed upon with the direct manager and should remain consistent week to week. Core collaboration hours are 10am to 3pm in the employee's local timezone.

## Home Office Requirements
Employees working remotely must have a dedicated workspace with a reliable internet connection of at least 25 Mbps. The workspace must be private enough to handle confidential conversations. Nexus HR is not responsible for home internet costs but provides a $50 monthly internet stipend for employees approved for full remote work.

## Equipment
Remote employees use company-issued equipment. Personal devices may not be used for work purposes unless explicitly approved by IT. Company equipment must be kept secure and must not be used by other household members.

## Full Remote Work
Employees may apply for full remote work after 12 months of employment. Applications are submitted through the portal under HR > Remote Work Request and require manager and HR approval. Full remote employees must be available to travel to the office up to 4 times per year for team events.

## Security Requirements
Remote employees must connect to company systems through the Nexus HR VPN at all times. Two-factor authentication is mandatory. Working from public wifi without VPN is prohibited. Any security incidents must be reported to IT immediately.
