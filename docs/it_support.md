# IT Support at Nexus HR

## Raising a Support Ticket
All IT issues must be reported through the Nexus HR portal under IT Support > New Ticket. Provide a clear description of the issue, the device affected, and any error messages you have seen. Screenshots can be attached to the ticket. Walk-in IT support is not available — all requests must go through the portal.

## Ticket Priority Levels
- **Critical:** System outage affecting multiple users or inability to access payroll. Response within 1 hour.
- **High:** Single user unable to work due to a technical issue. Response within 4 hours.
- **Medium:** Issue affecting productivity but workaround exists. Response within 1 business day.
- **Low:** General queries or non-urgent requests. Response within 3 business days.

## Password Reset
To reset your Nexus HR portal password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email. If you have lost access to your registered email, contact IT Support through a colleague's account or call the IT helpdesk at 1-800-NEXUSHR.

## Software Requests
Requests for new software must be submitted through IT Support > Software Request. Include the software name, business justification, and estimated cost. Software requests require approval from your department head before IT will action them. Standard software installations take up to 5 business days.

## Device Issues
For hardware faults, submit a ticket under IT Support > Hardware Issue. IT will assess whether the device can be repaired or needs replacement. Loaner devices are available for critical roles while repairs are underway. Do not attempt to repair devices yourself as this voids the warranty.

## Data Security
Never share your login credentials with colleagues. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact IT immediately through the portal or call the helpdesk. All employee devices are encrypted and must not be used to store company data outside of approved cloud storage.
