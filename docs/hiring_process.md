# Hiring Process at Nexus HR

## Opening a Role
To open a new position, the hiring manager submits a job requisition through the portal under Recruitment > New Requisition. The requisition must include the role title, department, salary band, and business justification. Requisitions require director approval for new headcount and HR approval for backfills.

## Job Posting
Once approved, HR posts the role on the Nexus HR careers page and selected job boards within 3 business days. Hiring managers can request specific job boards based on the role. All job postings must be reviewed by HR to ensure inclusive language and legal compliance.

## Application Review
Applications are managed in the Nexus HR portal under Recruitment > Applications. HR conducts an initial screen against the minimum requirements. Shortlisted candidates are shared with the hiring manager within 5 business days of the application closing date.

## Interview Process
The standard interview process consists of three stages:
1. **HR screen:** 30-minute video call with HR to assess culture fit and compensation alignment.
2. **Technical or skills interview:** 60-minute interview with the hiring manager and a team member.
3. **Final interview:** 45-minute interview with the department director.

All interviewers must complete unconscious bias training before participating in interviews. Interview feedback is recorded in the portal within 24 hours of each interview.

## Making an Offer
Offers are prepared by HR based on the approved salary band and candidate's experience. Verbal offers are made by the hiring manager after HR approval. Written offer letters are sent through the portal within 24 hours of verbal acceptance.

## Referral Program
Employees can refer candidates through the portal under Recruitment > Referrals. If a referred candidate is hired and completes 90 days of employment, the referring employee receives a $1500 referral bonus in the next payroll cycle.
