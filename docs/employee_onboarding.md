# Employee Onboarding at Nexus HR

## Overview
Welcome to Nexus HR. This guide walks new employees through the onboarding process from day one to full system access.

## Step 1: Account Setup
HR will send a welcome email to your personal email address within 24 hours of your start date. The email contains a temporary password and a link to activate your Nexus HR account. You must activate your account within 48 hours or the link expires and you will need to contact HR to resend it.

## Step 2: Profile Completion
Log in to the Nexus HR portal and complete your employee profile. Required fields include legal name, date of birth, emergency contact, tax information, and bank details for payroll. Incomplete profiles will delay your first paycheck.

## Step 3: Benefits Enrollment
New employees have 30 days from their start date to enroll in benefits. Navigate to the Benefits section in the portal and select your health, dental, and vision plans. If you miss the 30-day window, you must wait for the annual open enrollment period in November.

## Step 4: Equipment Request
Submit an equipment request through the IT section of the portal. Standard equipment includes a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Requests are fulfilled within 3 business days. For non-standard equipment, approval from your department head is required.

## Step 5: Team Introduction
Your manager will schedule a team introduction meeting within your first week. HR will also assign you an onboarding buddy — a colleague who will answer questions during your first 30 days.

## Step 6: Training Modules
All new employees must complete the following mandatory training modules within their first two weeks:
- Company values and code of conduct
- Data privacy and security
- Anti-harassment policy
- Role-specific tools training

Training is accessed through the Learning section of the Nexus HR portal. Completion is tracked automatically.
